Maviel ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Maviel app.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create an account or use the app:

  • Full name and email address
  • Children's names, ages, and dietary preferences (entered by you)
  • Meal plans you create within the app
  • Basic app usage data (screens visited, features used)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Generate and store personalised meal plans
  • Improve app features and performance

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may process your data:

  • Supabase — secure database and authentication (supabase.com)
  • PostHog — anonymous app analytics (posthog.com)
  • Anthropic Claude — AI-powered meal plan generation (anthropic.com)

None of these services sell your personal data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with any third parties except the services listed above, which are required to operate the app.

Children's Privacy

Maviel is designed for use by parents and caregivers. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. All children's data — including names and dietary preferences — is entered and controlled solely by the parent or caregiver account holder.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at swaha.work@gmail.com.

Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including encrypted storage and secure authentication provided by Supabase.
