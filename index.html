Our story

Why Maviel exists

Built from a real parenting problem — by a mother who lived it.

As mothers, it's almost instinct to be the one who handles food. I am no different.

I knew every meal, every texture, every thing my baby would and wouldn't eat. But I couldn't always be the one in the kitchen. I needed the people I trusted most to carry that with me.

My husband. My mum. Our nanny.

I didn't want to write long notes or answer texts mid-meeting. I just wanted something so simple, so easy, that anyone could follow it while I stepped away for a moment and actually breathed.

So I built Maviel. A baby meal planner that lets you plan meals well in advance and share them with every caregiver in your village in one tap. No confusion. No calls. Just confidence.

This is for every parent who loves fiercely, plans endlessly, and just needs a little help handing over the worry — not the care. 🧡
